Manufacturing Strengths

Precision Branding & Craftsmanship

We run state-of-the-art workshops utilizing multiple high-end branding and decoration methods to perfectly replicate your brand logo on any surface.

Screen Printing

Best for flat apparel, tote bags, and banners. Delivers vibrant, thick, and incredibly durable ink deposit that withstands hundreds of washes. Ideal for high-volume orders.

Suitable: Cotton, Polyester, Canvas

Premium Embroidery

Features premium, high-density stitch-work. Adds outstanding three-dimensional texture to caps, beanies, hoodies, jackets, and corporate uniforms. Long-lasting luxury finish.

Suitable: Heavy Knitwear, Caps, Canvas Bags

High-Precision Laser Engraving

Applies laser beams to cleanly etch away coating, revealing the raw material beneath. Unbeatable durability—does not peel or fade. Perfect for custom tumblers, keychains, and pens.

Suitable: Stainless Steel, Wood, Bamboo, Glass

Heat Transfer Printing

Enables high-definition full-color photographic print reproduction on sports jerseys, polyester athletic apparel, and complex graphic canvas bags. Sharp gradients and clear edges.

Suitable: Nylon, Polyester, Spandex, Cotton Blends

Precision Pad Printing

Uses high-quality silicone pads to print on highly irregular, curved, or recessed surfaces. Excellent for small electronic items, golf balls, custom toys, and multi-dimensional plastic goods.

Suitable: Plastics, Silicones, Curved Shapes

Luxury Foil Stamping

Applies heat and pressure to transfer premium gold, silver, or holographic metallic foil onto surfaces. Adds an ultra-premium luxury feel to gift boxes, paper bags, and leather notebooks.

Suitable: Coated Paper, Rigid Cardboard, PU Leather

Help Center

Frequently Asked B2B Questions

What format should I provide my brand logo in?

We strongly recommend vector file formats like .AI, .EPS, .PDF, or high-resolution .PNG with a transparent background. Vector formats ensure crisp laser etching and razor-sharp printing at any dimension.

Can I request physical pre-production samples?

Yes! While digital rendered proofs are completely free, we can also produce a physical pre-production sample for your absolute peace of mind. Standard sample fees are fully credited toward the bulk order deposit.

How are B2B payments protected?

We handle orders under official secure frameworks. Most clients prefer Alibaba Trade Assurance, which holds and protects payments until you verify shipping tracking. This completely eliminates international sourcing risk.

Do you handle shipping custom filings and duties (DDP)?

Absolutely. We offer complete Delivered Duty Paid (DDP) logistics. We handle export clearances, sea/air cargo freight, customs documentation, and domestic trucking—delivering straight to your company or Amazon warehouse door with zero hidden fees.
